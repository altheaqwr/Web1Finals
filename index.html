BIOGRAPHY

WhaleStoreXoxo

Love Pattranite Limpatiyakorn stars as "Wan" in the 2025 Thai GL series Whale Store xoxo (คุณวาฬร้านชำ) alongside Milk Pansa Vosbein. Premiering June 25, 2025, on GMMTV, the romance revolves around saving a grocery store, with Love playing a character managing the business. The show has gained popularity for its heartwarming story and high-profile pairing

TwentyThreePointFive

She also stars as Sun in the 2024 Thai GL series 23.5 (23.5 องศาที่โลกเอียง) alongside Pansa Vosbein (Milk) as Ongsa. As GMMTV's first GL series, it features Love as a popular student, Sun, who falls for her classmate, marking a major role in her career.

GirlRules

Love Pattranite Limpatiyakorn, stars as Gorya in the 2026 GMMTV Girls' Love drama Girl Rules. Her character is a stylist who plays hard-to-get, acting as the love interest for Shasha (played by Milk Pansa Vosbein), with their storyline featuring high-stakes romance, yearning, and a "bratty" side.
